Local, Family-owned, and Inclusive

At Pho 7, we want to offer the unique and distinct flavors of Vietnamese cuisine that can tailor to a variety of dietary restrictions. Our Pho soups are not only tasty, but also gluten-free and MSG-free. We also have many vegan options and low-carb substitutes available to cater to your diet. Just ask our servers for more details regarding the ingredients.







Fresh, healthy food that doesn't compromise Deliciousness

Our family visits the market every morning to bring you the freshest ingredient. Traditional Vietnamese food uses a lot of leafy greens, and we try to have a mix of different healthy vegetables in our reciep. Opt for a spring roll, a vermicelli salad bowl, or a banh mi if you are in the mood for a fresher change.
